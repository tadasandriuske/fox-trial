Founder and Lead Planner

Laura Vagonė

In event planning, the word 'impossible' is just the beginning of a great story.
In event planning, the word “impossible” is just the beginning of a great story.

Born in the small town of Jurbarkas, Laura Vagonė combines creativity and management skills honed through diverse work experiences, extensive travel, and a passion for reading. She brings a calm and reassuring presence, even in the most challenging moments. Laura is guiding principles include creating events as if for herself.

Now based in Vilnius, Laura enjoys reading, practising yoga, running, attending concerts and exhibitions, and savoring delicious food paired with wine and digestivo Espresso Martini.
